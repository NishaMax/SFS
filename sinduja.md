Future Expansion

Since you also want an owner management system, I would design the website now with future integration in mind.

Think of it as two connected applications:

1. Customer Website

This is what the public sees.

Beautiful branding
Categories
New arrivals
Offers
About us
Contact
WhatsApp
Branch details
Gallery
Language support 2. Owner Dashboard (Separate System)

Accessible only by login.

This would include:

📦 Stock management
➕ Add/Edit/Delete products
📊 Inventory levels
⚠️ Low-stock alerts
🧾 Supplier management
💰 Purchase records
📈 Sales reports
📷 Upload product photos
👥 Employee accounts (optional later)
📱 QR/Barcode support (future)
📤 Push updates to the website automatically

The website would simply display the data managed through this dashboard, so when the owner updates stock or adds new items, the public site reflects those changes without needing separate edits.

One suggestion I'd make is to keep the door-opening animation as a one-time experience. The first time someone visits, they see the branded entrance. After that, a cookie or local storage can remember their visit and take returning users directly to the homepage (unless they choose to replay the intro). This keeps the experience memorable without becoming inconvenient for regular visitors.
